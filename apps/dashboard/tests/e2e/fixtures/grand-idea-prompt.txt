An e-commerce platform for handmade ceramics that connects independent potters with collectors and interior designers. Sellers upload product photos, a clay-and-glaze provenance story, and shipping dimensions; the platform handles inventory, payments via Stripe, and a built-in commissioning workflow so a buyer can request a custom piece, lock the price, and track milestones (clay arrival → throw → bisque fire → glaze → glost fire → packed → shipped) with photo updates at each milestone. Discovery surfaces include curated collections, a "shop the studio" feed, and an AI-assisted matchmaker that pairs buyers with potters whose style matches their existing rooms (uploaded photos). Differentiator vs Etsy / Shopify: native commissioning, milestone escrow, and a curator review tier so designers can request vetted-only browsing. Target launch market: West Coast US + UK ceramics communities. Revenue: 8% take rate on stock sales, 12% on commissioned pieces, plus a $25/mo "Studio" tier for sellers with > 50 active listings.
