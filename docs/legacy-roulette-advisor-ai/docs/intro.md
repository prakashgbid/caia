# Introduction to Roulette Advisor AI

Welcome to the Roulette Advisor AI documentation. This site provides comprehensive information about the project's architecture, components, APIs, and development processes.

## What is Roulette Advisor AI?

Roulette Advisor AI is an intelligent roulette gaming platform that combines traditional roulette gameplay with AI-powered betting recommendations. The system analyzes historical game data and betting patterns to provide players with strategic betting advice.

![Roulette Game](https://example.com/roulette-preview-image.jpg)

## Key Features

### Interactive Roulette Board

Play European roulette with a beautifully designed interactive board that allows for all standard bet types:
- Straight (single number) bets
- Split bets
- Street bets
- Corner bets
- Line bets
- Dozen bets
- Column bets
- Even/Odd, Red/Black, High/Low bets

### Intelligent Betting Recommendations

Receive personalized betting recommendations based on:
- Statistical analysis of past results
- Pattern recognition
- Risk profile preferences
- Bankroll management strategies

The AI advisor provides not just recommendations but also the reasoning behind each suggestion, helping players make informed decisions.

### Comprehensive Bet History

Track your betting history with detailed analytics:
- Complete history of all bets placed
- Visual representation of outcomes
- Win/loss statistics
- Performance analysis by bet type
- Profit/loss tracking over time

### Statistical Analysis

Gain insights into the game through robust statistical tools:
- Hot and cold numbers identification
- Frequency distribution visualizations
- Outcome patterns analysis
- Probability calculations
- Expected value assessments

### User Management

Manage your profile and preferences:
- Secure authentication
- Customizable user settings
- Theme preferences
- Notification options
- Session history

### Bankroll Management

Take control of your virtual bankroll:
- Detailed transaction history
- Betting limits configuration
- Bankroll growth/decline analysis
- Risk assessment tools

## Technology Stack

The platform is built with modern technologies:

- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Material-UI
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express, MongoDB
- **AI Components**: TensorFlow.js, statistical models
- **Infrastructure**: Docker, Kubernetes (on GCP)

## Target Audience

Roulette Advisor AI is designed for:

- **Casual Players**: Who want to enjoy roulette with thoughtful guidance
- **Roulette Enthusiasts**: Looking to analyze their gameplay and improve strategies
- **Statistics Fans**: Interested in the mathematical aspects of casino games
- **AI/ML Enthusiasts**: Curious about practical applications of predictive modeling in games
