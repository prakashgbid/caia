## 4. DSPy compilation policy

`reviewPromptPattern()` recommends DSPy compilation when 2 of 3 signals hold:

1. **High-frequency call** (>1k calls/day) — compile cost amortises within a day.
2. **Reliability-sensitive** (>99% required accuracy) — manual prompts plateau ~95-97%.
3. **Cross-model portable** — DSPy compiles per-model; swapping is mechanically clean.

The orchestrator decides when to actually compile; the architect emits the recommendation only.
